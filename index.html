About QCFC

This is the official website for Queen City FC. Named after Denver, the Queen City of the West, Queen City FC is a new men's soccer team in the Denver Metro area coming in summer 2021. Looking to play Sunday soccer at a competitive level and then hit the brewery after? Then this is just the team for you.
